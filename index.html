📘 Lesson Topic

Present Continuous

O Present Continuous é usado para falar de ações que estão acontecendo agora, no momento da fala.

Audio 03 · Present Continuous
📖 I'm reading a new fantasy book this week.
🎨 She's painting a picture of the mountains.
🏀 We're playing basketball at the park right now.
🎬 They're watching a movie about surfing.
🎹 He's learning how to play the piano.
🍳 I'm cooking a new recipe for dinner.
🎶 We're listening to our favorite band.

Estrutura

Sujeito Verbo to be Verbo + -ing Complemento
Iamreadinga book
Sheispaintinga picture
Theyareridingtheir bikes in the park

Conjugando verbos no Present Continuous

Regra Geral

Adiciona -ing diretamente.

play → playing read → reading watch → watching
Termina em "e"

Tira o e, adiciona -ing.

make → making write → writing take → taking
C-V-C

Dobra a última consoante + -ing.

run → running sit → sitting swim → swimming
Termina em "ie"

Troca ie por y + -ing.

die → dying lie down → lying down
📕 Grammar

Part 1: Present Continuous × Hobbies

Audio 04 · Grammar Part 1
What are they doing?
to be + -ing = Present Continuous

Dave is listening to music.

Carol is writing.

Thales is doing yoga.

What are their hobbies?
Like / Love / Enjoy / To be into + -ing = Hobbies

Dave likes listening to music.

Carol loves writing.

Thales is into doing yoga.

These are my friends, Dave, Carol, and Thales. Dave is listening to his favorite song. He is into K-pop and dancing. Carol loves writing fantasy stories. She is writing a story about a dragon. Thales, on the other hand, is sitting on the ground. He enjoys doing yoga. I, myself, like playing chess and board games. It's pretty fun!

💬 Dialogue

Talking about Hobbies

Audio 07 · Dialogue
Lucas Hey, Carol! What do you like to do in your free time?
Carol Well… I love writing fantasy stories. It's so much fun!
Lucas That's cool! I enjoy playing chess and board games.
Carol Oh, really? I like board games too! Especially strategy ones.
Lucas Nice! Are you into any other hobbies?
Carol Yes, I am into painting a little bit. How about you?
Lucas I love listening to K-pop music and dancing. Let's shake, baby!
Sofia (laughing) Did someone say dancing? Count me in! I like salsa and bachata.
Lucas Wow, Sofia! You dance salsa? That's awesome!
Sofia Yes! And my brother plays the guitar, so sometimes we make a little music at home.
Carol That sounds fun! What about your mom, Sofia? What are her hobbies?
Sofia My mom loves gardening. She spends hours with her plants.
Lucas That's nice. My little brother is into video games. He can play for hours without moving!
Carol Sounds familiar! My dad enjoys cooking, especially Italian food.
Sofia Oh, yum! Can he cook pizza?
Carol The best pizza ever!
Lucas Great! We should all meet one day — pizza, board games, and dancing!
Sofia And maybe a little K-pop to finish the night!
