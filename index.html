📘 Lesson Topic

Present Continuous

O Present Continuous é usado para falar de ações que estão acontecendo agora, no momento da fala.

Audio 03 · Present Continuous
📖 I'm reading a new fantasy book this week.
🎨 She's painting a picture of the mountains.
🏀 We're playing basketball at the park right now.
🎬 They're watching a movie about surfing.
🎹 He's learning how to play the piano.
🍳 I'm cooking a new recipe for dinner.
🎶 We're listening to our favorite band.

Estrutura

Sujeito Verbo to be Verbo + -ing Complemento
Iamreadinga book
Sheispaintinga picture
Theyareridingtheir bikes in the park

Conjugando verbos no Present Continuous

Regra Geral

Adiciona -ing diretamente.

play → playing read → reading watch → watching
Termina em "e"

Tira o e, adiciona -ing.

make → making write → writing take → taking
C-V-C

Dobra a última consoante + -ing.

run → running sit → sitting swim → swimming
Termina em "ie"

Troca ie por y + -ing.

die → dying lie down → lying down
📕 Grammar

Part 1: Present Continuous × Hobbies

Audio 04 · Grammar Part 1
What are they doing?
to be + -ing = Present Continuous

Dave is listening to music.

Carol is writing.

Thales is doing yoga.

What are their hobbies?
Like / Love / Enjoy / To be into + -ing = Hobbies

Dave likes listening to music.

Carol loves writing.

Thales is into doing yoga.

These are my friends, Dave, Carol, and Thales. Dave is listening to his favorite song. He is into K-pop and dancing. Carol loves writing fantasy stories. She is writing a story about a dragon. Thales, on the other hand, is sitting on the ground. He enjoys doing yoga. I, myself, like playing chess and board games. It's pretty fun!

Comparative Adjectives

Curtos (não CVC)

Adjective + -er + than

tall → taller than fast → faster than
Curtos CVC

Dobra consoante + -er + than

big → bigger than hot → hotter than
Termina em -y

Troca y por ier + than

funny → funnier than
Longos (2+ sílabas)

more + adjective + than

expensive → more expensive than

Superlative Adjectives

Curtos

the + adjective + -est

fast → the fastest tall → the tallest
Longos

the most + adjective

beautiful → the most beautiful
Irregulares
good → the best bad → the worst
Audio 05 · Grammar Part 2
Brazil is bigger than Argentina.
Mount Everest is the highest mountain in the world.
Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world.
Messi is younger than Cristiano Ronaldo.
The cheetah is the fastest animal on land.
🗣️ Pronunciation

The "-g" in Present Continuous

Na fala, o -g final da forma -ing muitas vezes não é pronunciado. Isso é natural e casual — mas na escrita, sempre escreva completo.

Audio 06 · Pronunciation
I am studying English → "I am studyin' English" Eu estou estudando inglês
You are watching TV → "You are watchin' TV" Você está assistindo TV
He is playing soccer → "He is playin' soccer" Ele está jogando futebol
They are traveling to Spain → "They are travelin' to Spain" Eles estão viajando para a Espanha

✍️ Summary

In this lesson we covered…

🗣️

Hobbies

How to ask and answer about free-time activities using like, love, enjoy and to be into + -ing.

⏱️

Present Continuous

Actions happening right now: to be (present) + verb + -ing. Spelling rules included.

🔍

PC × Hobbies

"I am painting" (now) vs. "I like painting" (hobby). Different structures, different meanings.

📝

Adjectives

Descriptive adjectives, comparatives (-er / more) and superlatives (-est / most / the best / the worst).

🎤

Pronunciation

The dropped -g in -ing: "readin'", "watchin'", "playin'" — natural in speech, always written in full.
